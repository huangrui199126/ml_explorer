Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ML Explorer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. ML Explorer does not require an account, does not track users, and does not collect any personally identifiable information.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app caches the papers dataset locally on your device to allow offline access. This data consists entirely of publicly available academic paper metadata from Semantic Scholar. It is stored only on your device and never transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

The app fetches paper data from GitHub Pages (github.io). No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

Children's Privacy

ML Explorer does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
